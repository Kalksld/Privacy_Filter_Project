ABOUT OUR PRIVACY FILTER WEBSITE

Why We Created This Website

In today’s digital era, accidental sharing of personal information is common. This website was created to provide a safety net for users by scanning their content before posting. Our mission is to promote privacy-first digital habits.

What We Provide

Our platform detects personal data in both text and images—such as emails, phone numbers, and ID numbers—highlighting or masking it to ensure it’s safe to publish.

How It's Useful

Whether you’re a student, influencer, or everyday user, this tool provides peace of mind by helping you protect your digital identity and reduce exposure risks.
